ABOUT ME

Hi! I’m Lauren Schnapp, and I grew up in Westchester County, New York. From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by the way financial decisions impact people’s lives and how thoughtful planning can create freedom, opportunity, and peace of mind. That curiosity led me to the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where I’m a junior majoring in Personal Finance and Information Science with certificates in Business and Entrepreneurship. At UW–Madison, I’ve developed a strong interest in wealth management because it blends analytical thinking with relationship building which are two areas I really enjoy. I’m passionate about helping others feel confident about their financial futures and hope to build a career where I can make a meaningful difference. Outside of academics, I love staying active and connected. You’ll often find me trying new workout classes, exploring new places on long walks, spending time with friends and family, or hanging out with my dog, Da.
